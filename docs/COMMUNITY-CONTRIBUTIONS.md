# Community Contributions

The Rippled Field Guide is a community effort. This page recognizes everyone who has contributed operational insights, corrections, and improvements.

## How to Contribute

1. Open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/realgrapedrop/rippled-field-guide/issues) with your content, insight, or correction
2. Submissions are reviewed for accuracy and fit
3. Accepted contributions are incorporated into the Field Guide and credited here
